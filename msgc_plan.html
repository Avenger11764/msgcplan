Grand Finale Structure (Day 9)

Power Coins do not matter here. Only performance in these games determines the winner.

STAGE 1

The Culling

6 Players compete in Scribble.

The 2 players with the lowest scores are eliminated.

STAGE 2

The Semi-Finals

4 Players play Uno (Best of 3).

The first 2 players to secure a win advance.

STAGE 3

The Final Duel

2 Players face off in a Best of 3 Medley.

  • Game 1: @gamee High Score
  • Game 2: 31
  • Game 3: The Deck of 52 lives

The first to win two games is the Champion!
